The Black Mesa Research Complex is one of the world’s most advanced science centers, located in a vast underground facility in the New Mexico desert. Originally built in former missile silos, the complex has expanded significantly over the years to encompass multiple areas dedicated to cutting-edge research in physics, biotechnology, and other disciplines. Black Mesa is a highly controlled and secure environment where scientific innovations shape the future, but also a place where the unknown and the unexpected always lurk in the shadows of discovery.
